UX/UI Case Studies

Case studies
with user solutions.

Rabbit Advocates Responsive Website Redesign Rabbit Advocates is a local rabbit non-profit helping find loving homes for rabbits and educate the community about rabbit care. This project focused on creating an engaging experience for a user to adopt a rabbit companion. This will increase adoption rates and drive their passionate mission.
Department of Energy Responsive Website Redesign The Department of Energy strives to ensure America’s security and prosperity by addressing its energy, environmental and nuclear challenges. Their website hosts a lot of information and through this research and redesign we developed a more efficient navigation system to ease the uses stress.
Findflix Mobile App Design Findflix focuses on creating a streamlined experience for a user that uses streaming apps to watch movies, we all struggle with scrolling through available programs. Findflix filters and compiles all options in one spot.
